    • Welcome to Denari

    At Denari, we understand that paying rent every month is an important part of building a strong credit history. However, landlords choose not to report your rental data. That's why we've created a simple, secure platform that allows you to report your rental payments to TransUnion.

    • Improve Credit Score

      Many individuals who rent don't see their on-time rental payments reflected in their credit reports. Reporting this data can help build a stronger credit history, potentially leading to better financial opportunities in the future.

    • Unlock financial opportunities

      A better credit score opens up various financial opportunities. Users may be eligible for lower interest rates on loans, credit cards, and mortgages, allowing them to save money in the long run.

    • Overcoming Negative History

      If a user has had past credit issues or defaults, a positive rental payment history can demonstrate their improved financial behavior. It allows them to showcase their ability to manage financial responsibilities more responsibly, increasing their chances of loan approval despite previous setbacks.

    • Buy a Home

      When the time comes, a higher credit score makes you more attractive to mortgage lenders. Increasing your chances of getting approved for a mortgage to buy a home.

    For thousands of RentReporters’ customers, the average increase is 40 points. While we would love to be able to predict a specific score we simply can’t because the scoring algorithm the bureaus use is proprietary and not available to us.

    With Denari, we only report positive payment history. You can still use Denari if you’ve missed a couple rent payments or have been late paying rent. There is no credit check, no hard inquiry — just positive-only payment reporting and a 7-day money-back guarantee.

    Your roommate can use Denari too! All residents on the lease are eligible as long as there is a formal lease agreement (written or oral agreement), and they are on the lease. All users will be required to complete their own individual sign-up form with Denari.

    Great news. Your entire rent amount is eligible to be reported, not just your net payment. So make sure to provide the entire amount when you sign up, not just the portion you pay personally. For example, if your rent is $1,000 and $600 is subsidized for a net payment of $400, you would indicate $1,000 as your rent amount.
